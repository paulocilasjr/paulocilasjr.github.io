Graduated in Pharmacy (2011), Master in Biotechnology from the Biotechnology Center of the Federal University of Espírito Santo - UFES - (2013) and PhD in Biotechnology from RENORBIO/UFES (2018). Served as professor, accumulating over three year of experience.

My personal goal is to mechanistically understand the role of germline and somatic genetic factors involved in predisposition, initiation, progression, and response to therapy of diseases allowing the identification of better therapeutics and diagnostics strategies.

I envision my future research to span across some interrelated areas of regulatory mechanisms and gene expression. The unifying theme of the research will be the construction of genome mapping of regulatory elements involved in gene expression to each human cell type.
